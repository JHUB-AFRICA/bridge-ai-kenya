BRIDGE-AI
Kenya at JKUAT

Generative AI, IoT sensing and digital skills for climate-smart mushroom farming and rural innovation.

BRIDGE-AI Kenya documents JKUAT's contribution to the Horizon Europe BRIDGE-AI project. Through the JKUAT Smart Farm Zone and JHUB Africa, we explore how GenAI-enabled decision support, sensor data, digital shadows and inclusive training can help mushroom farmers, youth, women, developers and SMEs adopt practical digital agriculture solutions.

Why It Matters

Transforming Agriculture with AI

BRIDGE-AI Kenya addresses three critical areas for sustainable development

01
🌾

Climate-Smart Mushroom Farming

Using IoT sensors, GenAI analytics, and digital shadows to optimize growing conditions, reduce waste, and increase yields for Kenyan mushroom farmers.

02
📊

Inclusive Digital Skills

Empowering youth, women, and smallholder farmers with hands-on training in AI, IoT, and digital agriculture — building capacity for the future.

03
🧠

African GenAI Capacity

Building local expertise in generative AI for agriculture, creating a replicable model that can scale across East Africa and beyond.

JKUAT's Role

What JKUAT Is Doing

Leading the Smart Mushroom pilot and WP5 Capacity Building in Kenya

🍄 Pilot

Smart Mushroom Pilot

GenAI-assisted monitoring, anomaly detection, digital shadows, IoT sensing and farmer advisory tools for mushroom grow rooms at JKUAT Smart Farm Zone.

🤝 Living Lab

Kenya Living Lab

Co-design and feedback activities with farmers, researchers, youth, women, advisors and SMEs to ensure solutions meet local needs.

🎓 Training

Training & Bootcamps

Hands-on training programs, bootcamps, and SME mentoring to build digital skills and enable replication across East Africa.

📊 Mentoring

SME Mentoring

Supporting agricultural SMEs to adopt, adapt, and scale smart farming solutions through mentoring and technical assistance.

📚 Replication

Replication Toolkit

Developing playbooks, training materials, and open repositories to enable replication of the Smart Mushroom model across East Africa.

💡 WP5

WP5 Leadership

Leading Work Package 5: Capacity Building and Replication, building African expertise in GenAI solutions for agriculture.
