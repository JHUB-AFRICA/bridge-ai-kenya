Project Overview

BRIDGE-AI

BRIDGE-AI - Building ResIlient Development with GEnerative AI in Education & Agriculture is a Horizon Europe Research and Innovation Action that aims to improve African rural societies by integrating GenAI-based solutions into agricultural optimisation and digital skills acquisition.

The project operates across Kenya, Tunisia and Nigeria and develops use cases in mushroom cultivation, maize production, pasture prediction and pomegranate cultivation.

🎯 Project Goal

The project combines GenAI, Earth Observation data, IoT sensor measurements, digital shadows, semantic interoperability and low-bandwidth advisory interfaces. Its goal is to make context-specific agricultural decision support more accessible to farmers, agronomists, SMEs, developers and local innovation ecosystems.

JKUAT is the Kenyan beneficiary and leads Work Package 5: Capacity Building and Replication, anchoring the Smart Mushroom case study through the Mushroom Demonstration Farm at the JKUAT Smart Farm Zone in Juja, Kenya.

Objectives

What We Aim to Achieve

BRIDGE-AI works toward six primary objectives across research, capacity building, and replication

🔬

1. Technology Demonstration

Deploy and validate GenAI-enhanced Smart Mushroom system at JKUAT Smart Farm Zone with documented performance data.

🌾

2. Farmer Empowerment

Equip 400,000+ farmers across Kenya with smart farming capabilities, starting from Kiambu and Machakos counties.

👩‍🌾

3. Youth & Women Inclusion

Actively engage youth and women through targeted training, mentoring, and leadership pathways in agritech.

📚

4. Knowledge Transfer

Deliver modular training, bootcamps, SME mentoring, and replication resources for East Africa through WP5.

📈

5. Research & Evidence

Document pilot outcomes, lessons learned, and best practices for reporting to Horizon Europe and the public.

🌍

6. Scalability & Replication

Develop a replication playbook and toolkit for adoption across Kenya and East Africa.

Technology

How It Works

BRIDGE-AI combines multiple cutting-edge technologies to deliver practical solutions

GenAI Predictive Analytics

AI models predict yields, detect anomalies, and provide decision support for farmers and agronomists.

IoT Sensing

Real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, CO₂, light, and substrate moisture for precision agriculture.

Digital Shadows

Virtual replicas of grow rooms for "what-if" scenario testing, yield forecasting, and training simulation.

Semantic Interoperability

Using FIWARE/NGSI-LD standards for seamless data exchange between systems and platforms.

Low-Bandwidth Advisory

Voice alerts and SMS-based advisory in local languages (Swahili, French, Arabic) for farmers with limited connectivity.

Earth Observation

Satellite data integration for monitoring crop health, weather patterns, and environmental conditions.
