🚀 Adoption Pathway

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these phases to replicate the Smart Mushroom model in your context

01 📋

Assessment & Planning

Assess local needs, farmer readiness, and infrastructure. Identify key stakeholders and establish partnerships with local institutions.

📅 Phase 1: M28-M30
02 🔧

Setup & Implementation

Procure and install IoT sensors, configure climate control systems, deploy digital shadows, and set up low-bandwidth dashboards.

📅 Phase 2: M30-M32
03 🎓

Training & Capacity Building

Train farmers, youth, and women on IoT monitoring, AI advisory, and climate-smart mushroom farming practices.

📅 Phase 3: M32-M34
04 📊

Monitoring & Evaluation

Track yield improvements, farmer satisfaction, and economic impact. Iterate and refine the model based on feedback and data.

📅 Phase 4: M34-M36
